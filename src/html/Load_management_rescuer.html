Load Management Information

Welcome to the Load Management System. Here you can load items into your vehicle when you're within 100 meters of the base. Click 'Load' to select items from the base's inventory. The 'Unload' button transfers all items from your load back to the base's inventory.

Note: There's no limitation on the load each vehicle can carry.
