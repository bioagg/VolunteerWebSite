Welcome to Project Web 2024

Welcome to Volunteer, which specifically designed for communities impacted by natural disasters! This collaborative platform is a lifeline for citizens caught in crises like floods or earthquakes. It enables individuals to signal their urgent needs - be it for water, food, or medicine - or to offer surplus items for donation. Our system empowers rescue teams and volunteers to efficiently coordinate aid distribution directly from their vehicles, turning them into mobile support units. The app includes a dynamic map display, showcasing real-time requests and donations, allowing for targeted and timely responses. Additionally, our centralized 'rescue base' acts as a hub for managing resources and coordinating relief efforts.
